Color Palette:

Our color palette for the Study Spot Finder application has been thoughtfully curated to enhance the user experience, encouraging focus, inspiration, and productivity for students. Each color has been chosen with care, reflecting the essence of a perfect study environment.

Lavender Bliss

F1E3F3 - A calming lavender hue that mirrors the tranquility of a quiet study nook. It instills a sense of peace, making it an ideal choice for backgrounds and subtle accents, creating an atmosphere conducive to deep concentration.

Mystic Mauve

C2BBF0 - This subtle mauve shade embodies creativity and introspection. It's a gentle reminder that learning is a journey of discovery. Mystic Mauve is perfect for highlighting essential information, encouraging users to explore and learn.

Azure Dream

8FB8ED - The refreshing azure blue represents the vastness of knowledge and the endless possibilities of learning. Azure Dream instills a sense of openness and exploration, reminding users of the boundless horizons of education.

Sky Serenity

62BFED - A soothing sky blue that reflects the clarity of thought. It symbolizes trust, reliability, and the promise of a peaceful study environment. Sky Serenity helps users navigate the app with confidence, ensuring they find the perfect spot to focus.

Electric Blue

3590F3 - An energetic electric blue, reminiscent of inspiration and determination. This vibrant hue sparks enthusiasm and excitement, encouraging users to engage actively with the app. Electric Blue signifies the dynamic nature of learning, keeping users motivated throughout their study sessions.
